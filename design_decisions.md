Design Decisions: I added the ip_filter schema directly to the local copy of terraform-provider-google-beta/google-beta/services/storage/resource_storage_bucket.go in third_party/, because this is the fastest, safest, and most compatible approach to support the field without taking on the immense work of migrating the entire StorageBucket CRD to a Direct controller. I implemented standard flatten/expand functions to convert the terraform struct to the underlying googleapi structure. I generated the new CRD and the golang structures using the KCC provided scripts and added a new test fixture. The alternative, completely migrating StorageBucket to a Direct controller, was considered but deemed too large an undertaking for a single field addition.
