### [2026-06-15] VisionAIApplication Direct Types, CRD and Identity Implementation
- **Context**: Implementing direct types, CRD, and IdentityV2 for `VisionAIApplication` (Kind: `VisionAIApplication`).
- **Problem 1 (Reference of apiextensionsv1.JSON)**: In direct types generation, the generator places `ExperimentalConfig apiextensionsv1.JSON` inside `types.generated.go` without importing `apiextensionsv1`. Subsequent manual imports in `types.generated.go` are overwritten if the types are regenerated during `generate-all`.
- **Solution 1**: Added the manual import `apiextensionsv1 "k8s.io/apiextensions-apiserver/pkg/apis/apiextensions/v1"` in `types.generated.go`. Skipped `visionai` from regeneration inside `dev/tasks/generate-types-and-mappers` list to protect our manual import and prevent parsing compilation errors.
- **Problem 2 (Go Client Generation Name Mismatch)**: Running `make generate-go-client` maps the nested `ApplicationAnnotations` type of `google.cloud.visionai.v1.Application` to `ApplicationApplicationAnnotations`, but fields still refer to it as `ApplicationAnnotations`, causing compilation errors.
- **Solution 2**: Added a type alias `type ApplicationAnnotations = ApplicationApplicationAnnotations` under `pkg/clients/generated/apis/visionai/v1alpha1/visionaiapplication_types.go`. Handled this dynamically by inserting the patch via `sed` right after `generate-go-crd-clients` in `scripts/generate-go-crd-clients/generate-clients.sh`.
- **Impact**: Unblocks deepcopy and go-client generation for the `VisionAIApplication` resource, ensuring 100% pre-submit validation compliance.
